Baby StoneFruit

Connects your Pebble watch to Huckleberry through your Home Assistant.

Setup help
  1. Install the Huckleberry integration in Home Assistant via HACS, then add it under Settings → Devices & Services.
  2. Create a long-lived access token: Profile → Security → Long-Lived Access Tokens → Create Token.
  3. Find your child's device ID: Settings → Devices & Services → Huckleberry, click your child's device, and copy the device ID from the URL or device info.
  4. Use your HA URL exactly as you reach it from your phone (e.g. http://homeassistant.local:8123 at home, or your Nabu Casa URL anywhere).
No trailing slash. HTTPS required if accessing remotely. Generated under your HA profile. Stored only on your phone.
